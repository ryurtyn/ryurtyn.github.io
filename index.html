About Me

Hey, I'm Ruslana! I've recently completed my Masters of Engineering in Computer Science at Cornell Tech. Prior to that, I graduated from UC Berkeley in 2023 with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and a minor in Data Science (Berkeley AND Cornell? I'm really collecting the bear mascots).

At Cornell Tech, I had an incredible experience working on various advanced tech projects. I was the runner-up for the Startup Award with my startup, MercuryVote. I also delved into NLP research with Sasha Rush, focusing on using LLMs to extract information from large legal documents. Additionally, I explored computer vision and AI to extract procedural information from egocentric task-based videos at the XR Collaboratory. My coursework included a strong focus on Machine Learning, Computer Graphics, and Computer Vision.

During my time at UC Berkeley, I had the privilege of being a TA for the largest undergraduate course (1,500+ students!). I also served as the President of the Association of Women in EE&CS and championed diversity and inclusion as the DE&I Chair for Computer Science Mentors.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I've had some awesome internships at Amazon and the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency, where I worked on projects involving automation tools and machine learning.

Now, I'm getting ready to start a full-time position where I can continue to learn and apply my skills. When I'm not working on tech projects, you can find me sitting in a park reading a good fantasy novel, exploring the best food in NYC, and crafting (I like to knit and crochet).
