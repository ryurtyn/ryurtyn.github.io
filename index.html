About Me

Hey, I'm Ruslana! I've recently completed my Masters of Engineering in Computer Science at Cornell Tech. Prior to that, I graduated from UC Berkeley in 2023 with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and a minor in Data Science (Berkeley AND Cornell? I'm really collecting the bear mascots).

At Cornell Tech, I had an incredible experience working on various advanced tech projects. I was the runner-up for the Startup Award with my startup, MercuryVote. I also delved into NLP research with Sasha Rush, focusing on using LLMs to extract information from large legal documents. Additionally, I explored computer vision and AI to extract procedural information from egocentric task-based videos at the XR Collaboratory. My coursework included a strong focus on Machine Learning, Computer Graphics, and Computer Vision. My favorite class was Intelligent Autonomous Systems -- a class that touched on perception, control, planning, and learning in robotics.

During my time at UC Berkeley, I had the privilege of being a TA for the largest undergraduate course (1,500+ students!). I also served as the President of the Association of Women in EE&CS and championed diversity and inclusion as the DE&I Chair for Computer Science Mentors.

Now, I'm a Software Engineer at Amazon and I'm currently living in Seattle. Let me know if you have any Seattle recommendations for good food or fun events.
